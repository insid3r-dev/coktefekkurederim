CONTEMPLATION OF THE TRUTH

I have observed that a person who seeks to learn their religion in this age struggles to find a healthy and reliable source. Most available sources are presented through authoritative voices, often using an old style of language that makes them difficult to understand. Because of this, many readers become distant from the subject due to a monotonous style of explanation. Especially with the widespread influence of social media, reaching accurate information has become even more difficult. Therefore, my aim is not to provide long academic texts, but rather to create small sparks of reflection that draw attention to realities that are right before our eyes but often remain unnoticed. The entire work is written within the framework of the Qur'an, hadith, Sunnah and science. My goal is not to overwhelm the reader with academic references, but to use a clean, simple and clear language and to embark together on a journey of reflection that can be understood by people of different ages. I kindly ask you not to read these pages like a novel. Step back, read objectively and listen with your heart. The purpose should not be to argue, but to benefit. Unfortunately, although we hear many things around us, very few things truly reach our hearts.

Aslan ÇAKMAK

Biography

Aslan ÇAKMAK is an attorney who, after completing his legal education, continued his professional career in law. However, he has never limited learning only to his professional field. Over the years, his studies and research in various disciplines such as law, Islamic studies, psychology, neuroscience, history, philosophy, science, software development and art have played an important role in shaping his intellectual perspective. Adopting an interdisciplinary approach, ÇAKMAK aims to evaluate events from a holistic perspective by bringing together different areas of knowledge. This work is also a result of many years of research, observation and reflection.

Fields of Study

Law

He continues his professional work mainly in real estate law, private law and labor law. His experience in analytical thinking, problem solving and legal evaluation has provided him with a systematic perspective in his studies across different disciplines.

Islamic Studies

He has been regularly studying the Qur'an, hadith, the life of Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him), and classical Islamic sources for many years. He places importance on understanding religious matters within the framework of authentic sources and explaining them in a simple and accessible language.

Psychology and Neuroscience

His studies on human behavior, learning processes and decision-making mechanisms have contributed to his understanding of human nature and influenced the style of expression used in his works.

Science and Philosophy

He considers scientific developments and philosophical thought as complementary fields that contribute to understanding truth, and values establishing connections between different disciplines.

Art and Music

He is interested in digital art, music and literature, and believes that art is an important field that develops aesthetic perception, creativity and the ability to express ideas.

Software and Artificial Intelligence

He follows software development and artificial intelligence technologies, creates digital solutions and closely monitors technological developments.

The Creation and Purpose of the Book

Contemplation of the Truth is a work that emerged as a result of many years of reading, research, observation and reflection across different fields. However, the author does not attribute the creation of this book to his own knowledge, intelligence or abilities. On the contrary, whatever is correct and beneficial within these pages is regarded as a blessing and favor from Almighty Allah; while mistakes, shortcomings and inaccuracies are accepted as belonging entirely to himself.

Therefore, this work is not intended to grant prestige to its author or place his personality at the forefront. The author does not see himself as a creator of truth, but rather as a student who tries to convey certain truths that he has found meaningful, benefiting from the Qur'an, the Sunnah and authentic sources through a simple and understandable language. The owner of truth is not people, but Allah alone.

In this work, a simple and conversational style has been preferred instead of an academic and heavy language, so that readers of different ages can follow it comfortably. The purpose is not to provide ready-made answers, but to encourage the reader to reflect and reconsider realities that are always before our eyes but often remain unnoticed.

This book has been written with the belief that every benefit a reader gains from it is a blessing from Allah, and that the author only hopes to be a means for that benefit. For this reason, the author wishes this work to be evaluated not according to his own person, but according to the truth and benefit it contains.
